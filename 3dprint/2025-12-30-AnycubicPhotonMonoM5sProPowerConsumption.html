Anycubic Photon Mono M5s Pro - Power Consumption

Last Update: 30.12.2025

I was curious what power the Anycubic Photon Mono M5s Pro draws. What I measured:

  • Idle: 10 W (display on)

Some further measurement gave me some values for Anycubic White Standard Resin printing with the default profile.

  • 11 W while moving to initial position
  • 120 W while illuminating (relevant for: the first layers, which typically take more time)
  • 30 W while moving up/down after/before illumination this gives
  • 83 W on average while printing (3.6s illumination, 0.5s off, 2.6s moving time)
